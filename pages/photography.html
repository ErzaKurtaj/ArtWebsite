History of Photography

Sculpture, a timeless art form, began with ancient carvings like the Venus of Willendorf. In ancient Egypt, Greece, and Rome, it depicted gods and daily life in marble and bronze. The Renaissance brought a focus on realism with masters like Michelangelo. In the 20th century, sculpture embraced abstraction and new materials, evolving into diverse contemporary forms.
