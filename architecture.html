History of Architecture

Architecture has evolved from the simple huts of the Stone Age to the monumental pyramids of ancient Egypt, the classical temples of Greece and Rome, the towering cathedrals of the Gothic era, and the symmetrical beauty of the Renaissance. Modern architecture embraces functionality and innovation, showcasing sleek designs and new materials. Each era reflects humanity's creativity, technological advancements, and cultural shifts.
